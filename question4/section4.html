Video game genres - ratings and sales

Video games' ratings, number of release and sales between 2012 - 2016 (million copies/units)

Looking at the data between 2012 - 2016, we can see that 2013 tops the sales figures for games, whilst 2012 dominates the console sales in the period. This can be explained by the number of games released that year. 2016 experieced a dip in both games and console sales. Around this time, there are little to no Intellectual Properties (IPs) introduced, but rather remasters of games from the 80 and 90s of the previous century. Whilst nostalgic, it does not attract enough sales.

Console sales

Console sales from 2008 to 2018

Nintendo dominates the end of the last generation console with their Nintendo Wii and Nintendo DS models. They have also experienced a steady growth from their new handheld console Nintendo 3DS, but failed with the WiiU. However the newly introduced Nintendo Switch has helped Nintendo regained its position.

Sony lost its footing at the end of the last generation period (2008 - 2012). With the introduction of the PlayStation 4, sales has been increased dramatically. This is thanks to the exclusive games PlayStation 4 offers that dramatically increases sales from 2014 onwards

Microsoft had a steady sale throughout the period. Even when its latest console came out in 2013, it has been overshadowed by its rival Sony and their PlayStation 4

Console sales distribution from 2008 to 2018

This visualisation breaks down the number of units sold between 2008 -2018. We can see that handheld consoles (Nintendo DS, 3DS and Nintendo Switch have significant contributions). Sony also contributes to the visualisation thanks to its PlayStation 4 sales.

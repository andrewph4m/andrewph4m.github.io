Video game genres - ratings and sales

Based on the data from 2012 to 2016 regarding game sales and ratings by genre, we can see that Action games, while not highly rated than other genres, has a significant amount of games being released and sold than other genres. Stategy games have taken a dip in ratings in 2013, but quickly regained its ratings back in 2016, however does not regularly releases new games. This can explain due to the sarturated market, and a lot of exclusive games for consoles are still in development at this time (currrent generation consoles are roughly 1 - 2 years in its lifecycle)

Genre video game sales (in million copies) and ratings (based on professional critics and users' scores) by genres from 2012 to 2016

Over 5000 games were recorded that has a rating. Most games lies within the range of 45 - 75. And there is consistency between critics and user ratings across majority of games. However there are still some games that has high user ratings but low critic ratings. As observed in Metacritic in some games, due to backlash from gamers, there are some inconsistencies in rating, therefore we are able to detect which genre tend to have that pattern
